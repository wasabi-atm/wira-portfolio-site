About me

Hi, I’m Wira — a designer who codes. I care about how things look, feel, and work. My background in design helps me shape thoughtful visuals, and my love for technology lets me bring them to life.

Originally an international student, I’ve always been curious about how creativity and code can work together. These days, I enjoy building interfaces, experimenting with interaction, and crafting digital experiences that feel just right. Take a look around — this is where I share the things I’ve made, learned, or just found interesting.

What I'm Exploring Now

  • Web Design

    I’m learning the building blocks of the web to better understand how design translates into the browser. It’s satisfying to craft responsive layouts by hand and see how even small changes in CSS can completely shift the feel of a page.

  • Game Development with Unity

    I’ve been exploring Unity to bring interactive/game ideas to life — from movement mechanics to visual effects. It’s helping me understand how art, design, and logic come together in real-time environments.

    Drawing has always been a personal outlet, and lately I’ve been diving deeper into anime-inspired illustration. I’m focusing on characters, expressions, and color — trying to capture emotion in still frames.

    I’ve been experimenting with pixel art as a way to simplify and stylize my visuals. It’s a fun balance between creativity and constraint — and perfect for 2D game ideas I want to prototype quickly.
